Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Doney, created by Anusha Pasala, Founder & Developer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile app.

2. Information We Collect

Doney is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Activity Data: The activities you log (emoji, label, timestamp) are stored locally on your device only.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality (iOS/Android version).
  • No Personal Identifiers: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Location Data: We do not collect or track your location.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data stays on your device:

  • Local Storage: All activity data is stored locally using device storage.
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data is never uploaded to our servers.
  • No Analytics: We do not track your usage patterns or send analytics data.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your data with any third parties.

4. Data Security

  • Local-Only Storage: All data remains on your device.
  • No Network Transmission: Your activity data never leaves your device.
  • Device Security: Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features.
  • No Backend: We have no servers that could be compromised.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual activities or uninstall the app to remove all data.
  • No Account Required: Since we don't collect personal information, there's no account to manage.
  • Export: Your data is stored in a standard format on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Doney is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information, this is inherently protected.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at anusha55.pasala@gmail.com.
